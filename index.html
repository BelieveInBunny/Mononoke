Mononoke

Next Generation AnimeCMS & Forum that just works View me on GitHub

About

Mononoke is an Anime-Content-Management-System fully developed in PHP with the help of Bootstrap. It has a lot of features and focusses more on small little sites but can power big ones as well.

It is Open-source and free, available to everyone.

Also, please take note that the developers of this software are not responsible for what you do with it! If you use it for illegal stuff, the team is not to be held responsible!

The successor of H33Tx/aniZero which was discontinued in Januray 2022.

Features

All its features are listed below, but please keep in mind that it is still in development!

  • A fully integrated Account-System
    • Signing in & out
    • Confirming your eMail
    • Editing your Account-Preferences
    • Different User-Levels (Administrator, Moderator, User)
    • Viewing your own and other User-Profiles
  • A Forum-System that just works
    • Adding new Forums
    • Editing existing Forums
    • Setting Forums to Private or Public, Open or Closed
    • Creating new Threads and posting Replies
  • An Anime-System which you control
    • Adding Animes and editing them
    • Viewing an Anime and all its relations and Episodes
  • Episodes which you can track
    • Add and edit Episodes, supporting multiple Streamhosters
    • Track Episodes, mark as watched and add them to your Bookmarks
  • Commenting with Style
    • Comment on Animes and Episodes
    • Manage Comments, edit or delete them
    • BBCode Supported!
  • And more features...
    • A Watchlist you can add Airing Animes to
    • A Bookmark page you can share with others and track your Animes
    • And more features to come...
